Guidelines for Providing Effective Feedback

  • Be specific: Provide clear examples to support your observations.
  • Be objective: Focus on the course content and delivery rather than personal preferences.
  • Be constructive: Offer suggestions for how the course or teaching methods could be improved.
  • Be concise: Keep your feedback to the point to ensure it is easy to understand and act upon.
